Revenue Impact Breakdown

Affiliate revenue model: If 5% of 4,000 monthly visitors click a buy button (200 clicks), and 3% convert on the retailer site (6 purchases), at ~$5-10 average commission = $30-60/month from affiliates alone. As traffic grows to 10K+, this scales to $200-500/month.

Subscription revenue model: If 3% of 4,000 visitors start a trial (120 trials), and 10% convert to paid (12 subscribers × $2.99) = $36/month new MRR. Compounds monthly as subscribers stay.

Email remarketing: Captured emails get drip campaigns (when Listmonk is deployed). Email converts at 2-5x the rate of cold traffic. 300 emails × 5% convert = 15 additional subscribers = $45/month.
