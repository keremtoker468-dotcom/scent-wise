Endorsements vs. Reality

There’s a massive gap between what a celebrity puts their name on and what they actually spray on their wrists every morning. Rihanna may sell millions of bottles of her own line, but she’s been spotted wearing Maison Francis Kurkdjian and Kilian. Johnny Depp fronts Sauvage but has talked about his love for vintage Guerlain.

We dug through interviews, paparazzi shopping bags, and insider reports to find out what the biggest names in entertainment really keep in their fragrance wardrobes.

Why Celebrities Choose Niche Over Designer

A common thread in celebrity fragrance wardrobes is the lean toward niche houses like Le Labo, MFK, and Kilian. The reason is simple: exclusivity. When you can afford anything, you gravitate toward scents that aren’t on every department store shelf.

That said, classics like Chanel and Tom Ford still dominate many A-list collections. Quality is quality, regardless of price point or marketing budget.
