Best Tom Ford Lost Cherry Dupes & Alternatives 2026

Tom Ford Lost Cherry is a gourmand masterpiece of boozy cherry, almond, and tonka bean — but at $390+ per bottle, most of us need a more affordable option. Here are 9 alternatives that capture that intoxicating cherry-almond magic without the luxury markup.

By ScentWise · February 24, 2026 · 8 min read

What Makes Tom Ford Lost Cherry Special?

Released in 2018, Lost Cherry by Tom Ford is a unisex gourmand fragrance built around black cherry, bitter almond, cherry liqueur, tonka bean, and Turkish rose. The result is an intensely rich, boozy, cherry-forward scent that feels both playful and luxurious. It was an instant hit in the fragrance community, becoming one of the most talked-about cherry fragrances ever made.

The genius of Lost Cherry is the balance between sweetness and depth. The cherry and almond notes give it an almost dessert-like opening, but the tonka bean, Peru balsam, and roasted woody notes in the base keep it grounded and sophisticated. At $390+ for a 50ml bottle, it is firmly in the luxury category — which is exactly why the dupe market for this fragrance has exploded.

Cherry Fragrances: Beyond Lost Cherry

The cherry fragrance trend has exploded in recent years, and Lost Cherry is just the tip of the iceberg. If you love cherry scents, consider exploring the broader category. Cherry Punk by Room 1015 adds a leather edge. Lira by Xerjoff goes full dessert mode with caramel. Even budget options like Fragrance World Dark Cherry prove you do not need to spend hundreds to enjoy a great cherry fragrance.

The key difference between these dupes is the drydown. Lost Cherry's base of Peru balsam and roasted tonka gives it a smoky, almost campfire warmth that cheaper alternatives often simplify to vanilla. If that smoky base is what you love most, Cherry Punk and Dark Cherry come closest. If the fruity cherry opening is your thing, almost any option on this list will satisfy.
