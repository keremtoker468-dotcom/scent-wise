Trends in Celebrity Women’s Fragrance

Niche dominates the A-list. The most common brands in celebrity women’s collections are niche houses — Le Labo, Byredo, Kilian, and MFK. These brands offer uniqueness that mass-market designers can’t match.

Classic Chanel endures. Despite the rise of niche, Chanel remains the most-cited brand among celebrity women. Coco Mademoiselle, No. 5, and Chance are still in heavy rotation across Hollywood.

Gender boundaries are dissolving. Several women on this list wear traditionally “masculine” scents like Santal 33, proving that the best fragrance is simply the one that smells right on your skin.
