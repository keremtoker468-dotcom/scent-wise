Tips for Shopping on a Budget

Buy testers and unboxed. Tester bottles are identical to retail but sell for 20–40% less. The only difference is packaging.

Check travel sizes first. A 10ml travel spray of a luxury fragrance often costs $20–30 and lasts months with daily use. Great for sampling before committing.

Shop Middle Eastern brands. Houses like Armaf, Rasasi, and Lattafa produce incredibly high-quality fragrances at budget prices, often inspired by popular designer and niche scents.
