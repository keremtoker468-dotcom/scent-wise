Why Celebrity Fragrance Choices Matter

There is a reason "what perfume does Rihanna wear" is one of the most searched fragrance queries online. Celebrities set trends, and their fragrance picks often signal broader shifts in what is considered desirable. When Rihanna made Love Don't Be Shy by Kilian go viral, it introduced an entire generation to niche perfumery.

But here is the truth: a fragrance that smells incredible on one person might not work for you. Body chemistry, skin type, and even diet affect how a perfume develops on your skin. That is why it is important to find scents that match your profile, not just copy someone else's.

How to Find Your Own Signature Scent
